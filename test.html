What do we do?


At the heart of "Joy Bites" is a commitment to:


  • Quality: We source only the freshest ingredients to prepare meals that are both healthy and flavorful.

  • Nutrition: Our menus are crafted with the help of nutrition experts to ensure each meal meets the dietary needs of growing children

  • Sustainability: We prioritize eco-friendly practices and strive to minimize our environmental impact throughout our operations.

  • Innovation: We continually seek out new recipes and ideas to keep meals exciting and enjoyable for kids.

  • Community: Building strong relationships with schools, parents, and
    children is a core part of who we are. We value feedback and actively engage with our community to improve our services.

About Us

Welcome to "Joy Bites", an Organisation dedicated to providing wholesome, delicious meals for little ones. Founded by two passionate mothers, Renu and Prachi, our mission is to supply schools with nutritious meals that kids love, while supporting their growth and development.


As parents, we understand the challenges of finding the perfect balance between convenience, taste, and nutrition in meals for children. Our journey began with a shared vision of creating a solution that would simplify life for parents and schools while ensuring that kids receive wholesome, tasty meals every day.
